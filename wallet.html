How to Create a TON Wallet
√ó
1
Choose a TON Wallet

There are several TON wallet options available, each with different features:

  • Tonkeeper - Most popular, user-friendly mobile wallet
  • OpenMask - Browser extension similar to MetaMask
  • Tonhub - Mobile wallet with additional features

We recommend Tonkeeper for beginners.

2
Download & Install

Download your chosen wallet from the official sources:

Tonkeeper:

For OpenMask, visit openmask.app and add the extension to your browser.

3
Create a New Wallet

After installing the app:

  1. Open the wallet app
  2. Select "Create new wallet"
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions
  4. IMPORTANT: Write down your recovery phrase and keep it in a safe place. This is the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access.
4
Fund Your Wallet

To use your TON wallet, you'll need to add some TON coins:

  • Buy TON directly through the wallet app
  • Transfer TON from an exchange like Binance, OKX, or ByBit
  • Receive TON from another wallet
5
Connect to Jio

Now that you have a wallet:

  1. Return to Jio's wallet page
  2. Click "Connect TON Wallet"
  3. Select your wallet from the list
  4. Approve the connection request in your wallet

You're all set to use TON features with Jio!

How to Connect Your TON Wallet
√ó
1
Get Started
Welcome to Jio Meme Coin! To use TON blockchain features, you'll need to connect your TON wallet. This quick tutorial will guide you through the process.
2
Click Connect Wallet
Start by clicking the "Connect TON Wallet" button on this page. This will open a list of compatible wallets.
3
Choose Your Wallet
Select your preferred wallet from the list. Popular options include Tonkeeper, OpenMask, and TonHub. If you don't have a wallet yet, we recommend installing Tonkeeper.
4
Approve Connection
Your wallet will ask you to approve the connection request. This is a security measure to ensure you're aware of which sites can access your wallet.
5
Connected!
Once approved, you'll see your wallet address and TON balance displayed on the page. You can now send TON coins and interact with Jio features!
6
Try It Now
Ready to connect? Click "Connect Wallet Now" to start the process, or close this tutorial and click the "Connect TON Wallet" button on the page when you're ready.
